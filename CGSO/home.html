CGSO is the chemistry graduate student organization at Virginia Commonwealth University. As an organization, we strive to connect graduate students receiving degrees in chemistry, chemical engineering, chemical biology, chemical physics and nanoscale science. In addition to facilitating networking amongst our peers, we also encourage our members to have a strong presence at local and national conferences promoting the outstanding chemistry going on at VCU. In addition to our academics, we also focus on science outreach for our community. This year our organization is tutoring students at Church Hill Activities and Tutoring, which focuses on providing education and life skills to underprivileged children in the Church Hill area. We tutor students in all areas they need help including not only science and math, but also reading and foreign languages. We recently participated in the 6th Annual Fall Harvest Festival that was held at the Siegel Center offering safe, fun activities for Halloween. At Fall Harvest Fest we had activities that allowed students to participate in Halloween themed chemistry demos and offered Wint-O-Green lifesavers to demonstrate luminescence when bitten.

If you know of projects in the area that you think we would be interested in, please contact Natalie Herring (herringnp@vcu.edu) or Alex Martin (martinad3@vcu.edu) as we are continuously looking for new ways to be involved!
